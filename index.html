Publication

Health Monitoring System Based on LightGBM, CatBoost, and Explainable Boosting Machine (EBM)

Yashvvinie Santhakumar, Aishwarya Yenmanagandla; Amruthavarshini Sriram, Prithvi Pothupogu

This paper introduces a health monitoring system utilizing vital parameters such as pulse rate, body temperature, blood oxygen saturation (SPO2), and respiratory rate. The system employs advanced machine learning algorithms, including CatBoost, LightGBM, and Explainable Boosting Machine (EBM) classifiers, with the ensemble algorithm serving as the primary model. This approach aims to enhance the accuracy and interpretability of health condition predictions, facilitating early diagnosis and personalized treatment recommendations.

DOI: 10.1109/WCONF61366.2024.10692251
